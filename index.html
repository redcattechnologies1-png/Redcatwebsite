Got Questions?

Frequently Asked
Questions

Everything you need to know about our products and services. Can't find the answer? Reach out to us.

What is your 42-point quality check?

Our quality check includes a comprehensive inspection of hardware and software. We check battery health (must be 85%+), screen calibration, button responsiveness, speaker quality, and network connectivity among other parameters.

How do I claim my warranty?

Claiming warranty is simple. Log in to your account, select your order, and click 'Claim Warranty'. Our team will coordinate the pickup of your device within 24 hours.

Do you offer international shipping?

Currently, we ship across India and select regions in South Asia. We are working on expanding our global footprint very soon. Stay tuned for updates!

What is your refurbishment process?

Every device goes through deep cleaning, internal component verification, part replacement (if necessary with original parts), and rigorous testing before being certified for sale.
